Agenda

Accessibility in design

Learn what are key principles and best practices for creating digital products that are usable by everyone, regardless of their physical or cognitive abilities. The lecture will explore various aspects of accessibility, including color contrast, keyboard navigation, screen readers, and other.

Design systems in Figma

Explore how to create and maintain a design system in Figma. Discover best practices for organizing components, creating documentation, and ensuring consistency across your designs. Learn how to leverage Figma's collaboration features to enhance teamwork and streamline the design process.

Usability testing

Discover how to conduct effective usability testing to identify user pain points, improve user experience, and optimize product design. Learn about different testing methods, tools and techniques, and how to analyze test results to make informed design decisions.
